How it all began

By Natasha Adu Moses

Date: 20 Aug 2021

Time for a short story… Grab a cuppa ☕

At the beginning of 2020, my contract with my previous employer ended, and I was left feeling like it was time that I committed myself to a fulfilling career path that offered me longevity and satisfaction. I knew that it was essential for me to work in an area that also encouraged the use of my creative skills. One day, while I was job searching, I came across an article about Web Development. Hmmm, I thought… Let me explore this area a little more. I then visited the ‘Holy Grail of Knowledge’ aka YouTube and found a host of tutorials and advice videos that confirmed that joining the tech world was 100% for me. Excited, I headed to Udemy (recommended by YouTube, of course) and my journey as a 'Techie' began.

Since my technological epiphany, I have been on an accredited course, where I gained a Level 2 Certificate in IT User Skills in Open Systems and Enterprise. I also attended an online Bootcamp with Cheetah Codes Academy to brush up on my web development skills. At this point, I can safely say that I have extensive knowledge about the fundamentals of HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. I have also learned to use UI frameworks such as Bootstrap and JavaScript frameworks such as React.

What am I up to now? Well, I recently started the Google UX Design course where I am learning about UX Design principles. I'm loving the course so far and it is putting web development into perspective for me. All of a sudden, everything makes perfect sense! I suddenly understand why a website's CTA is where it is, why it is that colour and why it is not where I think it would look 'prettier' (no pun intended...web devs get the reference...Ha!). After completing the first three courses, I now know how to conduct UX research, create storyboards, wireframes & much more. UX design feels like home to me. I'm glad that I embarked on this tech journey. The future is very bright!
