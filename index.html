How it all began

By Natasha Adu

Date: 05 June 2021

Time for a short story… Grab a cuppa ☕

At the beginning of 2020, my contract with my previous employer came to an end, and I was left feeling like it was time that I committed myself to a fulfilling career path that offered me longevity and satisfaction. I knew that it was essential for me to work in an area that also encouraged the use of my creative skills. One day, while I was job searching, I came across an article about Software Development. Hmmm, I thought… Let me explore this area a little more. I then visited the ‘Holy Grail of Knowledge’ aka YouTube and found a host of tutorials and advice videos that confirmed to me that this was indeed the career path that I had been looking for. Excited, I headed to Udemy (recommended by YouTube, of course) and my journey as a Web Developer began.

Since my technological epiphany, I have been on an accredited course with a coding Bootcamp where I was awarded a ‘Level 2 IT User Skills in Open Systems and Enterprise’. I have also taken several courses on Udemy and created a few projects that have helped me step into my new career. I have learned and absorbed the fundamentals of HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. I have also learned to use UI frameworks such as Bootstrap and JavaScript frameworks such as React.
